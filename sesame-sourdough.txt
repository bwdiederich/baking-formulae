Sesame Sourdough 
(after Tartine, this blog http://www.theperfectloaf.com/a-return-to-basics/, and me)

Flour 100%
Water 75%
Salt 2%
Starter 250%
1 Cup toasted unhulled sesame seeds

1000g flour total
20g rye
100g ww
880g ap

750 H20 (around 80 degrees)

20g sea salt

250 g starter (12 hours after feeding, equal parts flour/water, 50/50 ww/ap flour)

to toast seeds - on cookie sheet, 400, 20ish minutes, check and shift around every 8 to 10 minutes
